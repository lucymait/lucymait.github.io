About

Hi! My name is Lucy and I am a Software Engineer based in London seeking new opportunities. Throughout my life, I have been proud of my ability to solve problems creatively. In my previous roles, I was able to optimise processes and create solutions for my company and co-workers. Whilst I was proud of what I achieved, as a digital native I knew there was always a better digital solution, and if the product was not already out there, I wanted to be able to build it.

My desire to push solutions to be the best, compelled me to enroll on General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive so that I could put into practice the skills I sought, and maximise my ability to be a creative problem solver. I would like to take on the challenge of working as a junior developer, doing something I love for a business I am passionate for, with the opportunity to further my skills and work with and learn from leaders in the tech community.
